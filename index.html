About

Hey! I'm Rohan

I've always been drawn to the challenge of solving complex problems—whether it's optimizing an algorithm, designing an AI model, or figuring out why my code won't compile at 2 AM. Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's in Computer Engineering at NYU Tandon, building on my Computer Science degree from NMIMS University. Over the years, my curiosity for mathematical modeling, AI, and system design has fueled my work on projects ranging from predictive analytics in e-learning to Bezier curve motion planning for autonomous navigation.

My capstone project focused on making AI more intuitive and accessible, where I designed an automation agent using Hugging Face models and the Electron framework—a project that won 2nd place at my university’s Annual Project Competition. I also got hands-on experience at Anvitech Solutions, where I worked on full-stack development and cloud-based architectures, contributing to an open-source Hospital Information Management System.

Right now, I'm expanding my expertise in machine learning and neural networks through an AI certification at IIT Madras. Whether it's building scalable applications, refining AI models, or exploring embedded systems, I’m passionate about creating technology that’s both powerful and accessible.
